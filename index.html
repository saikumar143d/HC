3. 6-Month Preparation Roadmap

1

Month 1: Foundation Building

  • Focus on General Knowledge (AP State-specific) and Basic English grammar.
  • Start reading "The Hindu" or “Eenadu” daily.
  • Use NCERT books for history and geography basics.
2

Month 2: Practice Begins

  • Start practicing Reasoning and Arithmetic.
  • Enroll in an online test series for regular practice.
  • Begin computer fundamentals if unfamiliar.
3

Month 3: Skill Development

  • Focus on Typing and Stenography practice.
  • Write one mock test weekly.
  • Practice previous year question papers.
4

Month 4: Advanced Practice

  • Solve sectional tests to improve weak areas.
  • Enhance speed and accuracy.
  • Continue following current affairs regularly.
5

Month 5: Full-Length Mock Tests

  • Attempt 3-4 full-length mock tests weekly.
  • Review answers carefully and identify weak topics.
6

Month 6: Revision & Final Preparation

  • Focus on thorough revision of all topics.
  • Write daily mocks and refine your exam strategy.
  • Prepare and finalize all documents for verification.
